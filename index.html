Luxify App

Luxify App is a software service that tracks Louis Vuitton item stock and sends notifications to you as soon as they become available to ✨ Add to Cart ✨.

It currently sends notifications through Facebook Messenger Chat and supports Louis Vuitton stock notifications.

Tips and Tricks for Securing Items 💡

  1. Turn message notifications from Luxify App on
  1. Stay signed in to your Louis Vuitton account
  1. Pre-fill your delivery address details so you can check out faster
  1. Pre-fill your credit card information or use PayPal so you can check out faster
  1. Refresh from your Wishlist page - there are rumours that items will appear as "Add to Cart" a few seconds sooner on there than on the product page
  1. If an item is in stock, the notification system will message you continuously (every 15 seconds) until it is out of stock. This will give you an idea of how quickly that item sells out
  1. If you manage to add the item to your cart but cannot checkout due to the item being sold out already, you can continue refreshing your cart until you may proceed, or wait until the next Luxify App notifications to proceed

FAQ 🤓

Q: Why does Luxify App use Facebook Messenger to send notifications?

A: The simple answer - it's fast. If you require accommodations or would prefer to receive notifications through emails, please let us know. We're happy to see what we can do!

Q: Does Luxify App only work for Louis Vuitton items? What about items from _______?

A: At the moment, we only confidently support notifications for Louis Vuitton items (across all of their international sites). However, we are looking into adding support for Chanel Beauty and other designers. Feel free to ask if you have something specific in mind and we can look into it for you.

Q: Will Luxify App help me complete my purchase of items?

A: Nope! This is very important to note - Luxify App is a notification service that will let you know when you'll be able to add an item to your cart, but it will not check out or automate the buying process for you.

Q: I got a notification but the item was out of stock when I went to check. Why didn't it work?

A: Depending on the item, it can sell out within seconds to minutes after it stocks. We would recommend staying signed in to your Louis Vuitton account so you can quickly add it to your cart as soon as you get a notification.

Q: I added the item to my cart but then I couldn't proceed! Help?!

A: Remain calm! Keep refreshing from your cart until you are able to proceed, because items can come back into stock when people abandon their carts. Alternatively, you can try to checkout later once you get another in-stock notification for it.

Q: If I add the item to my cart, will it be removed if I don't check out?

A: To our knowledge, no. If you have the item in your cart already, you can refresh from your cart instead of from your Wishlist/the product page.

Q: I haven't gotten a notification in days. What's going on?

A: Louis Vuitton stock is unpredictable, and there have been times when a specific item didn't stock for weeks. For example, the Mini Pochette in Monogram barely stocked at all in the first two weeks of October 2020. Be patient! The advantage of using Luxify App is that you will be the first to know when it does stock 😇
